## Ecosystem Integration
- **iPhone:** Seamless integration with Apple devices and services (Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, AirPods, HomeKit).
- **Android:** Functional across diverse devices and brands, experience varies by manufacturer; relies on Google services (Drive, Photos, Assistant).

## Cost and Value
- **iPhone:** Premium pricing, high resale value, fewer models, predictable performance.
- **Android:** Wide price range from entry-level to flagship, flexible for all budgets.

## Conclusion
- **Choose iPhone**: If you value long-term software support, privacy, ecosystem integration, consistent performance, and resale value.
- **Choose Android**: If you prioritize customization, variety, affordability, innovative features, and flexible ecosystems.
Both platforms support advanced AI, robust app stores, and excellent cameras, so lifestyle, budget, and current devices should guide the final choice.
