| Enterprise | Custom | - Dedicated support manager<br>- Uptime SLA<br>- BYO Cloud<br>- 24/7/365 Premium support<br>- Private Slack channel | องค์กรขนาดใหญ่, workload สำคัญ |

Compute Pricing
- Micro: $10/เดือน (1 GB RAM, 2-core ARM)  
- Small: $15/เดือน (2 GB RAM)  
- Medium: $60/เดือน (4 GB RAM)  
- Large: $110/เดือน (8 GB RAM)  
- สูงสุด 64 cores / 256 GB RAM ($3,730/เดือน)

Supabase Pricing Plans (May 2026)
| Plan | Monthly Cost | Key Features | Best For |
|------|--------------|--------------|----------|
| Free | $0 | - Unlimited API<br>- 50,000 MAUs<br>- 500 MB DB<br>- 1 GB Storage<br>- 5 GB Egress<br>- Community support | Hobby projects, prototypes |
| Pro | $25+ | - 100,000 MAUs (extra billed)<br>- 8 GB DB (extra billed)<br>- 250 GB Egress<br>- 100 GB Storage<br>- Email support<br>- Daily backups (7 days)<br>- Log retention 7 days | Production apps, SaaS startups |
| Team | $599+ | - SOC2, ISO 27001<br>- HIPAA add-on<br>- SSO Dashboard<br>- Priority support + SLA<br>- Daily backups (14 days)<br>- Log retention 28 days | Companies needing compliance |
| Enterprise | Custom | - Dedicated support manager<br>- Uptime SLA<br>- BYO Cloud<br>- 24/7/365 Premium support<br>- Private Slack channel | Large-scale, mission-critical workloads |

Compute Pricing
- Micro: $10/month (1 GB RAM, 2-core ARM)  
- Small: $15/month (2 GB RAM)  
- Medium: $60/month (4 GB RAM)  
- Large: $110/month (8 GB RAM)  
- Up to 64 cores / 256 GB RAM ($3,730/month)
