Privacy Policy for Vaulted

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Vaulted with TikTok Login, we collect: your TikTok username and profile picture for display purposes only. We temporarily process product images you upload to generate prompts. Images are not stored permanently on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

Your TikTok profile info is used only to display your name after login. Uploaded images are used only to analyze product category and generate text. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. TikTok Data

We request `user.info.basic` to show your name and `video.publish` to post videos when you click the button. We do not access your followers, messages, or other private data.

4. Data Storage

Vaulted is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not have a database. Your TikTok access token is stored only in your browser's local storage and is deleted when you log out.

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We only use local storage to remember your TikTok login session.
